# FAQ

## General

### What is LEVERAGED?
LEVERAGED is a decentralized leveraged trading platform on BSC that lets you amplify your crypto exposure up to 5x.

### How is it different from perpetuals?
- No funding rates
- Only pay fees on profits (25%)
- Simpler UX - no perpetual mechanics

### Is it safe?
Smart contracts carry inherent risks. We recommend starting small and only using funds you can afford to lose. See [Risk Factors](../security/risks.md).

---

## Trading

### What assets can I trade?
Currently: BTC, ETH, and BNB. More coming soon.

### What's the maximum leverage?
5x leverage is the maximum.

### How are prices determined?
We use Chainlink decentralized oracles for accurate, manipulation-resistant pricing.

### What happens if I get liquidated?
You lose your deposited collateral. The protocol repays your debt and the liquidator receives a bonus.

### Can I add collateral to avoid liquidation?
Yes! Go to your position and click "Add Collateral" to improve your health factor.

### What's the health factor?
A measure of position safety. Above 1.5 is safe, below 1.1 risks liquidation.

---

## Fees

### What fees do I pay?
- **Entry fee:** 0.1% on deposit
- **Value fee:** 25% of profits (only if profitable)
- **Gas fees:** Small BSC transaction fees

### What if my position loses money?
You pay $0 in value fees. Only the 0.1% entry fee was charged.

### Can I reduce fees?
Yes! Stake $LVG to get up to 25% off the value fee.

---

## Lending

### How do I earn yield?
Deposit USDT to the lending pool. Traders borrow your funds and pay interest.

### What's the APY?
Variable based on utilization. Higher utilization = higher APY.

### Can I withdraw anytime?
Yes, subject to available liquidity. If utilization is 100%, you'll receive funds as borrowers repay.

### Is my deposit safe?
Deposits are secured by borrower collateral. However, smart contract risk exists.
